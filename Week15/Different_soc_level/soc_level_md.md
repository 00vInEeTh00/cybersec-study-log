## 1. Tier 1: SOC Analyst (L1) -- Alert Monitoring & Triage

### Main Role:

First-level monitoring and alert handling.

### Responsibilities:

-   Monitor SIEM dashboards and alerts.
-   Perform initial triage of alerts.
-   Identify false positives.
-   Escalate real threats to Tier 2.

### Skills:

-   Basic understanding of networking and security.
-   Familiar with SIEM tools (e.g., Splunk, QRadar).
-   Strong attention to detail.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Tier 2: Incident Responder (L2) -- Deep Investigation & Response

### Main Role:

Investigation and incident response.

### Responsibilities:

-   Analyze escalated alerts.
-   Perform forensic analysis of logs, endpoints, and network traffic.
-   Confirm security incidents.
-   Coordinate with IT to contain and mitigate threats.

### Skills:

-   Strong knowledge of malware, exploits, and lateral movement.
-   Scripting/automation (Python, PowerShell).
-   Use of tools like Wireshark, VirusTotal, etc.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Tier 3: Threat Hunter / SME (L3) -- Advanced Analysis & Threat Hunting

### Main Role:

Advanced threat analysis and proactive security.

### Responsibilities:

-   Proactively hunt for undetected threats.
-   Develop detection rules.
-   Reverse engineer malware.
-   Provide expert guidance to Tier 1 and Tier 2.

### Skills:

-   Deep understanding of APTs and TTPs (MITRE ATT&CK).
-   Threat intelligence integration.
-   Advanced forensics and malware analysis.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. SOC Manager

### Main Role:

Management and coordination of SOC operations.

### Responsibilities:

-   Supervise SOC team members.
-   Provide technical guidance when required.
-   Hire, train, and evaluate staff.
-   Create and manage operational processes.
-   Review incident reports.
-   Develop crisis communication plans.
-   Manage SOC budget and financial aspects.
-   Support security audits.
-   Report to CISO or senior management.

### Skills:

-   Leadership and project management.
-   Cybersecurity certifications (e.g., CISSP, CISM).
-   Good understanding of compliance standards (ISO, NIST, etc.).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Collaboration Between SOC Tiers for Effective Security Operations

Security Operations Center (SOC) teams work collaboratively using a
tiered workflow where each level supports the next.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tier 1 Investigation and Incident Response

### Collaboration Role:

-   Review incidents escalated from Tier 1.
-   Perform in-depth analysis using threat intelligence.
-   Determine attack scope and affected systems.
-   Coordinate with IT and system teams to contain threats.
-   Develop remediation and recovery strategies.
-   Escalate highly complex incidents to Tier 3 when necessary.
-   Provide feedback to Tier 1 to improve alert accuracy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tier 3 Coordination and Oversight

### Collaboration Role:

-   Coordinate communication between tiers.
-   Establish workflows and escalation procedures.
-   Review incident reports and performance.
-   Provide resources and training.
-   Communicate with executive management.
